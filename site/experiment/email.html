Story Analysis Online Experiment

Call for Participation

15th - 26th November 2021

£20 Amazon eGift Voucher reward on completion





Reading and writing are inevitable parts of learning in academic life. Any tools based on research archives that improve learners’ creativity and learning experience can become an essential part of online education. Are you a teacher, student or staff in Goldsmith, University of London? We are cordially inviting you to take part in Story Analysis Online Experiment. Participants will perform reading comprehension and writing synthesis based on terms presented via various visualizations and text representation which is automatically extracted from a text archive. Participants need to use a computer/laptop and browse using Chrome during the experiment session. For more details about the experiment, please visit Call for Participation.


We are aiming to recruit 32 participants on a first-come-first-served basis. The Story Analysis Experiment will take place from 15th November 2021 to 26th November 2021. Each working day there will be 4 sessions. Each session will be 90 minutes long. After successful sessions, participants will be rewarded a £20 Amazon eGift Voucher.


Please book a session.


Thank you very much for your time.
